1️⃣ Search Products

Engineers can search construction materials based on the Site’s District and Pincode. This ensures that only locally available suppliers and agencies are shown.

You can filter products by category, price range, and availability. This helps in selecting materials that suit the site's budget and project requirements.

2️⃣ Add to Cart

After selecting required products, engineers can add items to the site-specific cart.

Each site will maintain its own cart to ensure materials are tracked separately for different projects. Quantities can be modified before sending quotation requests.

3️⃣ Quotation & Order Requests

Engineers can request quotations from suppliers for the selected cart items.

Suppliers will respond with pricing and availability. Engineers can review quotations and approve or reject based on budget and project timeline.

4️⃣ Confirmed Orders

Once quotation is approved, orders move to the Confirmed Orders section.

Here engineers can track delivery status, payment mode, dispatch updates, and completion records for each site.
