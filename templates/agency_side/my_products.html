📦 Product Management Dashboard


Manage all your construction materials easily using the tabs above. This section helps agencies organize products, add variants, and keep inventory updated for engineers searching materials.


🔹 All Products

View all the construction products you have added such as cement, steel, bricks, sand, and other materials. You can also check product stock availability and variants.


➕ Add Product

Add a new construction product to your catalog by entering product details like brand name, product name, description, and image.


🧩 Add Product Variations

After creating a product, you can add multiple variants such as size, weight, price, and stock availability. This helps engineers choose the exact material they need.


✏ Edit & Remove Product

Update product details, modify variant information, or remove products and variants from your catalog whenever required.


💡 Tip: Keep your product stock updated regularly so engineers can see accurate material availability.
